Latest Government Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

This is a crop insurance scheme that provides insurance coverage and financial support to farmers in the event of crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

This scheme aims to expand cultivated areas with assured irrigation, improve water use efficiency, and promote sustainable agriculture practices

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

This scheme provides financial assistance to states for developing agriculture and allied sectors, focusing on sustainable agriculture practices and infrastructure development.

National Food Security Mission (NFSM)

This mission aims to increase the production of rice, wheat, pulses, coarse cereals, and commercial crops through area expansion and productivity enhancement.

Soil Health Card Scheme

Under this scheme, soil health cards are issued to farmers, providing information about nutrient status and recommended dosages of fertilizers for their farmland.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

This scheme promotes organic farming and supports clusters of organic farmers with organic certification, marketing, and branding assistance.

National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

This mission promotes sustainable agriculture practices, such as soil health management, water management, and integrated pest management.

National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

This is an online trading platform for agricultural commodities, facilitating better price discovery and transparent marketing channels for farmers.

Agricultural Marketing Infrastructure(AMI)

This scheme aims to develop agricultural marketing infrastructure, including setting up of rural markets, storage facilities, and processing units.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

This scheme provides income support to small and marginal farmers by providing direct cash transfers of ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

This is a pension scheme for small and marginal farmers, providing a monthly pension of ₹3,000 after attaining the age of 60 years.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY-Per Drop More Crop)

This component of PMKSY focuses on improving water use efficiency through micro-irrigation techniques like drip and sprinkler irrigation.
